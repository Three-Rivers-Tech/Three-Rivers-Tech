# Product Overview

## Three Rivers Tech Website

This is a professional IT services business website transformation project. The goal is to convert the current website from casual, inconsistent messaging into a credible, professional business presence.

## Key Objectives

- Transform casual language into professional, business-appropriate content
- Establish consistent brand messaging across all pages
- Replace unrealistic statistics with credible, achievable metrics
- Create detailed service descriptions that demonstrate expertise
- Build trust through realistic case studies and testimonials

## Target Audience

Business decision-makers and companies seeking IT services, including:

- Small to medium businesses needing technology solutions
- Companies requiring custom software development
- Organizations seeking IT infrastructure support
- Businesses looking for SaaS integration and support

## Core Services

- Enterprise Software Solutions (custom development)
- IT Infrastructure & Support
- SaaS Solutions & Integration
- Hardware Support & Maintenance

## Brand Voice

Professional, approachable, and expert-focused. Avoid casual language like "boring tasks" or "normal people" in favor of business-appropriate terminology that demonstrates competence and reliability.
